Welcome to Rosebud World

Declaration of Principles

This is a community-driven, open-source platform for sharing news, opinions, and articles. Inspired by the spirit of free speech in Citizen Kane, we uphold the following principles:

  • We do not censor opinions that follow our simple legal guidelines.
  • Articles can be anonymous or credited—your choice.
  • We celebrate freedom of expression without judgment of beliefs.

Legal Rules

  • No posting of illegal images.
  • No direct calls for anyone to harm themselves or others.
  • No incitement to violence or forced acts.

How to Submit Articles

We currently accept submissions in the following ways:

  1. Email: Send your article as text or an attachment to [email protected].
  2. IPFS: Upload your article to IPFS using your preferred method or ipfs.tech, then email us the CID. We will review and pin if approved.

Once approved, articles are pinned on our IPFS node and listed here.

Note: If you'd like to help keep content online and censorship-resistant, feel free to pin any approved articles on your own IPFS node.
